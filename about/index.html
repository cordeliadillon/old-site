Hey there! I'm Cordelia!

I enjoy collecting hats shaped like food, playing Boggle, and circumambulating cities.

My pronouns are she/her.

I'm a cartoonist.

I've got an MFA in Comics from California College of the Arts. I mostly draw autobio webcomics and am slowly working on my first book, a comic memoir about anxiety. I also illustrate most of my slides for my public speaking engagements. I'm super into comics that are accessible to blind and low vision readers.

I'm a techie.

Professionally, I'm a web accessibility engineer, most recently working on accessibility at Salesforce and Dropbox. I'm passionate about making technology a great experience for everyone. I love helping older adults improve their computer skills and doing tech talks on accessibility and universal design.
